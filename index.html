Our Mission...

To make the Truck hiring process easier and faster [Seamless] for Businesses, individuals and organizations.

Our Vision...

To be a trusted and reliable truck hauling platform.

Our Objectives

  • To link users [Traders, organizations, individuals] with a wide variety of affordable and good quality trucks.
  • To increase efficiency in the trucking industry.
  • To cut the costs associated with trucking. E.g wastage that have increased costs of doing business.
  • To promote trade in Uganda, the region and in the continent.
